Delivery Information

Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods:

  • UPS11: Estimated delivery time is 1-2 business days.
  • UPS2: Estimated delivery time is 2-5 business days.
  • UPS3: Estimated delivery time is 5-10 business days.
  • FREE: Estimated delivery time is 1-2 weeks.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the total weight of your order and the shipping method selected during checkout. The exact shipping cost will be displayed before you complete your purchase.

The delivery time depends on the shipping method you choose and your location. Please note that delivery times may vary due to unforeseen circumstances, such as weather conditions or holiday seasons.

We offer international shipping to many countries. However, please be aware that customs fees, taxes, and import duties may apply when your package reaches the destination country. These additional charges are the responsibility of the customer.

Due to certain restrictions, we are unable to ship to some countries or regions. If your country is not available for selection during checkout, we apologize for the inconvenience caused.

Please ensure that you provide the correct and complete delivery address when placing your order. We will not be held responsible for any delays or delivery issues due to incorrect or incomplete addresses.
