Returns Policy

30-Day Return Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with an item, you can return it within 30 days of receiving your order.

Conditions for Returns

To be eligible for a return, please ensure that the item:

  • Is unused, unworn, and in the same condition as when you received it.
  • Is in its original packaging, including all tags and labels.
  • Does not fall under any non-returnable or non-refundable categories (e.g., underwear, personalized items, etc.).

Return Process

If you wish to return an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support team within 30 days of receiving your order to initiate the return process.
  2. Provide your order number and the reason for the return.
  3. Our team will guide you on the next steps, including the return address and any additional information required.
  4. Package the item securely and include the original invoice or packing slip.
  5. Ship the item back to us using a trackable shipping service. Please note that the return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.
  6. Once we receive the returned item and verify its condition, we will process your refund or exchange as per your request.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email notification regarding the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method used during checkout. Please allow a few business days for the refund to reflect in your account.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or style, please contact our customer support team. We will assist you with the exchange process, subject to availability.

Need Further Assistance?

If you have any further questions regarding our returns policy or need assistance with a return, please feel free to contact our customer support team. You can reach us by visiting the Contact Us page on our website or by sending an email to gymshark@gmail.com. We're here to help!
